Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butterin a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
